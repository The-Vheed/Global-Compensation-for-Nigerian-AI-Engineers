A Proposal for Fair and Strategic Compensation

Based on the comprehensive data and market realities presented, a compensation package that reflects the global value of AI engineering expertise and aligns with international remote work standards is both reasonable and strategic.

Aligning with Global Value

As indicated by platforms like Crossover ($5,000-$8,333/month), Arc.dev ($4,471-$4,634/month), and Himalayas.app ($6,083/month), experienced Nigerian AI engineers working remotely for international organizations often earn compensation that reflects their global demand. A salary of $5,000 USD monthly is consistent with these established benchmarks.

A Mutually Beneficial Investment

Engaging highly skilled Nigerian AI talent at a globally competitive rate (e.g., $5,000+ monthly) remains a resourceful approach for any organization compared to acquiring equivalent expertise in higher-cost markets. This represents a strategic investment in talent that is beneficial for both the organization and the engineer.

Recognizing Specialized Expertise

The skills of an AI engineer contribute directly to an organization's global objectives. The value an engineer brings is tied to the international scope of their work. Therefore, it is appropriate for compensation to reflect this global value rather than being based solely on the local economic conditions of their residence.
